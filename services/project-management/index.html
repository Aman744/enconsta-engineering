01 / OVERVIEW

Vertical Overview

Our Project Management and Controls division keeps projects on track, on budget, and within compliance. We deploy project managers, scheduling engineers, cost controllers, document controllers, and quality managers to establish baseline controls. We monitor progress using Primavera P6 and Earned Value Management (EVM), identify scheduling risks early, and coordinate project deliverables across engineering and site contractors.

Business Value Delivery

We improve schedule reliability and keep project budgets on track. Our earned value metrics identify cost and schedule variances early, allowing project directors to make course corrections before they impact deadlines.

Key Differentiators

  • Strict schedule tracking with integrated resource weighting.
  • Clear progress reporting utilizing S-curves and Earned Value Management (EVM).
  • Cloud-based document control systems ensuring version control across stakeholders.

06 / CASE STUDY

Execution in Practice

Solar Project Schedule Recovery

The Challenge

A 100MW solar park was facing a 6-week delay in foundations and module deliveries, risking steep grid penalties.

Our Approach

We rebuilt the Primavera schedule, identifying non-critical tasks that could be fast-tracked. We optimized crew rosters and coordinated daily delivery priorities with manufacturers.

The Result

The project team recovered 5 weeks of delay, commissioning the solar park within the contract window and avoiding liquidated damages.

08 / QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Earned Value Management (EVM)?

It is a project management methodology that integrates cost, schedule, and scope to measure project performance and predict outcomes.

How do you manage drawing revisions?

We use document control platforms to issue, log, and track drawings, ensuring outdated revisions are marked void.

Can you provide S-curve progress reports?

Yes, we generate weekly S-curves showing planned vs. actual progress for engineering, procurement, and construction.

How do you handle project risks?

We compile and maintain a project risk register, rating impact and probability, and detailing mitigation actions.
