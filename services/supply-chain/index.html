01 / OVERVIEW

Vertical Overview

Our Procurement and Supply Chain division secures the materials, equipment, and components required to build complex industrial projects. We manage the complete procurement lifecycle, including global sourcing, vendor pre-qualification, bidding, technical bid evaluations, materials expediting, quality inspections, and complex international logistics. By partnering with leading manufacturers worldwide, we guarantee compliance, quality, and on-time delivery.

Business Value Delivery

We lower procurement costs by 15% through global bulk sourcing contracts and pre-negotiated vendor agreements. Our strict material expediting and quality inspection loops ensure 99.8% material trace-compliance, reducing schedule delays due to incorrect materials or transit bottlenecks.

Key Differentiators

  • Global network of pre-audited fabricators and material suppliers.
  • Dedicated shop expediting teams placed in major industrial manufacturing hubs.
  • Digital tracking of material test reports (MTRs) and shipping manifests.

06 / CASE STUDY

Execution in Practice

Duplex Piping Procurement Program

The Challenge

An offshore desalter project required specialized Duplex stainless steel piping components under a tight 16-week timeline, but standard mill lead times exceeded 30 weeks.

Our Approach

We bypassed stockists and coordinated directly with three European mills, securing capacity by leveraging existing bulk quotas. We placed an in-shop expeditor to monitor manufacturing progress daily.

The Result

All Duplex pipes and fittings were delivered to the fabrication yard in 14 weeks, avoiding a projected 3-month project delay and maintaining platform launch schedules.

08 / QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure material traceability?

We enforce strict custody tracking, mapping every piping component and fitting back to its original heat number and Mill Test Certificate (MTC).

What QA/QC steps are taken during procurement?

Our inspectors perform audits, witness manufacturing milestones, and review QA manuals before releasing shipments.

Do you handle international shipping of oversized cargo?

Yes, our logistics team plans heavy-transport operations, including barge transports, multi-axle trailers, and permits.

What is your vendor dispute resolution process?

We maintain clear service level agreements (SLAs), performing prompt non-conformance reports (NCRs) and coordinating replacements directly.
